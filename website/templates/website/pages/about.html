Our Story

ForgeForth Africa was born from a simple observation: Africa has an abundance of brilliant, skilled professionals who remain invisible to the global job market — not because they lack talent, but because they lack access.

We saw talented developers passed over because their CVs weren't "polished enough." We saw skilled nurses overlooked because they didn't have the right connections. We saw potential go unrealized because the system wasn't built to see it.

So we decided to build something better. A platform that sees beyond the CV, that understands skills in context, and that connects African talent with opportunities across the continent and beyond.
